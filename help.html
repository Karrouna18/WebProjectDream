Common Issues

Here are some of the common issues that users face and their solutions:

  • Unable to access my watchlist?
    Make sure you are logged in with the correct account. Also, check your browser settings to ensure there are no conflicts with cookies or JavaScript.
  • Movies not loading properly?
    Clear your browser's cache and cookies. If the issue persists, try switching to a different browser or updating your current one.
  • Subscription or payment issues?
    Contact support for assistance with billing, subscription plans, or any payment-related concerns.
