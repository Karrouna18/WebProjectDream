Your Privacy Matters

At DreamFlix, we are committed to safeguarding your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to improve your DreamFlix experience, including:

  • Personal Information: Account details such as your name, email address, and subscription preferences.

  • Device Information: Data about the device and operating system you use to access DreamFlix.

2. Data Security

We take data security seriously. All personal information is stored using industry-standard encryption methods to ensure your data remains safe. We will never sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Managing Your Data

You have the right to access, update, and delete your personal data at any time. To manage your preferences, simply visit your account settings. If you wish to delete your account, please contact us.

4. Cookies

DreamFlix uses cookies to enhance your experience on our platform. These cookies help us remember your preferences and provide personalized content recommendations. You can manage your cookie settings in your browser.
