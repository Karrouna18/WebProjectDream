How We Use Cookies

Cookies help us improve your experience by remembering your preferences and settings. We use different types of cookies for various purposes, as described below:

1. Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the proper functioning of the DreamFlix website. Without these cookies, certain features, such as account login and video playback, would not be available.

2. Performance Cookies

Performance cookies help us understand how users interact with the platform by collecting anonymized data. This data allows us to improve the functionality of the website.

3. Advertising Cookies

Advertising cookies help us provide relevant ads and offers to you. These cookies track your browsing habits across websites and help us serve personalized ads based on your interests.

4. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have the option to control which cookies are set by adjusting your browser settings. You can block cookies entirely, or clear your cookie history at any time.
